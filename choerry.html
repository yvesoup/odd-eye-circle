Choerry


Choerry (Hangul: 최리) is the eighth revealed member of LOONA and a member of its second sub-unit, LOONA / ODD EYE CIRCLE. She was born as Choi Ye-rim (Hangul: 최예림) on June 4, 2001 in Bucheon, South Korea. She debuted on July 12, 2017 as a member and subsequently released her solo single album Choerry on July 28, 2017.
