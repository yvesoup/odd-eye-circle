JinSoul


JinSoul (Hangul: 진솔) is the seventh revealed member of LOONA and a member of its second sub-unit, LOONA / ODD EYE CIRCLE. She was born as Jeong Jin-sol (Hangul: 정진솔) on June 13, 1997 in Dongdaemun District, Seoul, South Korea. She debuted on June 13, 2017 as a member and subsequently released her solo single album JinSoul on June 26, 2017.
